Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay
  • Digital Wallets: Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, Amazon Pay
  • UPI: All UPI-enabled apps
  • Net Banking: All major banks supported
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for orders under ₹50,000
  • EMI Options: 3, 6, 9, 12 months EMI available
All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels to ensure your financial information remains safe.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Order Modification: You can modify your order (change address, add/remove items) within 30 minutes of placing it by contacting our customer support.

Order Cancellation:
  • Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they are shipped
  • Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled but can be returned
  • COD orders can be refused at the time of delivery
  • Refunds for prepaid orders are processed within 3-5 business days
To modify or cancel, visit "My Orders" in your account or call our customer support at +91 123 456 7890.
How do I get an invoice for my order?
Invoices are automatically generated for all orders:
  • Email Invoice: Sent to your registered email within 24 hours
  • Digital Invoice: Available in "My Orders" section for download
  • Physical Invoice: Included with your shipped order
  • GST Invoice: Available for all orders above ₹500
For business purchases, you can add your GSTIN details during checkout for proper tax invoicing.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping options and delivery times?
We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:
  • Standard Delivery (Free): 4-7 business days (Free on orders ₹999+)
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days (₹99)
  • Priority Express: Next business day (₹199)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore (₹299)
  • Scheduled Delivery: Choose your preferred date and time slot
Delivery times may vary during peak seasons and for remote locations. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.
Do you deliver to my location?
We deliver across India to most PIN codes:
  • Metro Cities: Same-day and next-day delivery available
  • Tier 2 Cities: 2-4 business days delivery
  • Tier 3 Cities & Towns: 4-7 business days delivery
  • Remote Areas: 7-14 business days (additional charges may apply)
Enter your PIN code on the product page to check delivery availability and estimated delivery time. Some products may have location restrictions due to shipping regulations.
How can I track my order?
Tracking your order is easy with multiple options:
  • SMS Tracking: Automatic updates sent to your mobile
  • Email Updates: Detailed tracking info sent to your email
  • My Orders: Real-time tracking in your account dashboard
  • WhatsApp: Get updates on WhatsApp by messaging us
  • Tracking Number: Use the tracking ID on courier website
You'll receive your tracking number within 24 hours of order confirmation. Track your package every step of the way from warehouse to your doorstep.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
Our hassle-free return policy ensures customer satisfaction:
  • Return Window: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unused, with original tags and packaging
  • Free Returns: No questions asked for defective or wrong items
  • Easy Process: Initiate returns through your account or customer support
  • Quick Pickup: We'll schedule a pickup from your address
Non-returnable items: Personal care products, intimate wear, customized items, and perishable goods cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.
How long does it take to process refunds?
Refund processing times depend on your payment method:
  • Digital Wallets: Instant to 24 hours
  • UPI/Net Banking: 1-3 business days
  • Credit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • COD Returns: Bank transfer within 7-10 business days
You'll receive email confirmation once your refund is processed. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Yes! We offer convenient exchange options:
  • Size Exchange: Free size exchanges within 15 days
  • Color Exchange: Exchange for different color (subject to availability)
  • Product Exchange: Exchange for similar priced items
  • Instant Exchange: Same-day exchange in metro cities
  • Price Difference: Pay extra or get refund for price difference
Exchange process is faster than return+repurchase. We'll arrange pickup of the old item and delivery of the new item simultaneously when possible.

Account & Privacy

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is quick and easy:
  • Sign Up: Click "Sign Up" and enter your email/mobile number
  • Verification: Verify via OTP sent to your email/phone
  • Profile Setup: Add your name, address, and preferences
  • Social Login: Quick signup with Google, Facebook, or Apple ID
  • Guest Checkout: Shop without account, convert later
Benefits of having an account: Order tracking, wishlist, exclusive offers, faster checkout, and personalized recommendations.
How secure is my personal information?
We take your privacy and security seriously:
  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted with 256-bit SSL
  • Secure Storage: Personal data stored in encrypted, secure servers
  • No Data Sharing: We never sell your data to third parties
  • PCI Compliance: Payment processing meets international security standards
  • Regular Audits: Security audits conducted by third-party experts
  • GDPR Compliant: Full compliance with data protection regulations
You can view, update, or delete your personal information anytime through your account settings.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Resetting your password is simple:
  • Step 1: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  • Step 2: Enter your registered email address or mobile number
  • Step 3: Check your email/SMS for reset link or OTP
  • Step 4: Click the link or enter OTP to create new password
  • Step 5: Choose a strong password and save changes
Password Tips: Use at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information or common passwords.
